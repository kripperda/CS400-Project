Kory Ripperda

Kory Ripperda is an Intellectual Property Strategy Specialist at The Boeing Company and is pursuing software engineering deggree at Bellarmine University. With three young children with way too many toys, he understands that children go through sets of toys and on to the next thing very quickly. With the quality of educational toys out there today, he knows it is important to make sure each child has the chance to have "too many toys" to help them learn, play, and use their imagination. Also, he is seeking a consolidated place to get rid of the toys that have overtaken his home.

Megan Cole

Megan Cole is a software engineering student at Bellarmine University with a background in elementary education. She has seen firsthand the importance of toys in supporting cognitive development, problem-solving, and physical skills in primary-aged students. Megan is passionate about combining her skills to help make toys more accessible to families, schools, and non-profits, helping to ensure that children have the resources they need to grow and learn.
