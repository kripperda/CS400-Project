User Stories - Scenario 3

As a future grandparent, I need to gather cheap or free toys for my grandbaby.

As a parent, I want to be able to support my children with learning toys for their child.

As a nurse, I know the importance of toys that are safe and not recalled.

As a neighbor, I would like to be able to pass down toys to others in need around me.

As a neighbor, I would like donated toys and learning activities to be close to me.

As a parent with grown children, I would like to have an aggregated place to donate my children's old items.

As a future grandparent, I want to know if there are resources for other grandparents to do activities with their grandchildren.

As a future grandparent, I would like donation places to be able to list toys they have for donation on a single site.
